/help — prints this message

/stock_list — prints <i>all</i> available stocks

/my_portfolios — prints list of all your portfolios

/new_portfolio <code>NAME</code> — creates a new portfolio <i>with initial balance of $10'000</i>

/buy <code>PORTFOLIO_ID</code> <code>TICKER</code> <code>QUANTITY</code> — buys some stock for one of your portfolios

/sell <code>PORTFOLIO_ID</code> <code>TICKER</code> <code>QUANTITY</code> — sells some stock from one of your portfolios

/new_contest <code>NAME</code> <code>HOURS</code> — create a new contest with given name (one word only allowed) that lasts for specified number of hours

/my_contests — prints list of all the contests you have joined

/join_contest <code>CONTEST_ID</code> — join contest with specified id

/join_contest global — join default global contest

/short <code>PORTFOLIO_ID</code> <code>TICKER</code> <code>QUANTITY</code> — borrow requested stocks for 24 hours and immediately sell them
